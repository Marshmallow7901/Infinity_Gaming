Privacy Policy

Last updated: 05 May 2026

Overview

Infinity Gaming values your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it in this demo storefront experience.

What We Collect

  • Profile details you enter such as name, email, and support requests.
  • Cart, wishlist, checkout notes, and subscription preferences stored in your browser.
  • Anonymous usage data generated by your interactions with pages and features.

How We Use Data

  • To display and persist your account, cart, and wishlist state.
  • To improve user experience and customer support workflows.
  • To provide optional updates when you subscribe to offers.

Storage

This website uses browser local storage for demo functionality. Data remains on your device unless you clear browser data.

Your Choices

You may clear saved data at any time by clearing site storage in your browser settings.
