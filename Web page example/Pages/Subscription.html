Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does the free trial work?

You get 7 days of full access to your chosen plan at zero cost. No credit card is required to activate the trial. If you decide not to continue, simply cancel before the 7 days are up and you won't be charged anything.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can switch between Starter, Pro Gamer, and Elite at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

How do championship entry tickets work?

Entry tickets are credited to your account on the 1st of each month. Pro members receive 2 tickets; Elite members get unlimited access to all championship events. Unused tickets do not roll over to the next month.

What is the monthly game box?

The game box is a curated selection of digital game keys from our catalogue, chosen based on your favourite game preferences. Pro members receive 2 titles; Elite members receive 3 titles per month.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by visiting your account settings or by contacting us via the Contact page. There are no cancellation fees and you retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Are championships open to all skill levels?

Absolutely. Community tournaments welcome all skill levels. As you progress, you'll earn ranking points that qualify you for higher-tier championships. Everyone starts somewhere — the Infinity Gaming community is welcoming to all.
